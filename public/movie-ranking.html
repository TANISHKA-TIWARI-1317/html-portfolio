The Best Dramas According to Tanishka

My top 3 dramas of all time

Hidden love

This is a chinese drama with plot of falling in love with brothers bestfriend. The main leads are very a beautiful couple. This drama also shows a brother sister bond. It is a very wholesome drama.

Queen of Tears

This is a korean drama with rich bossy ceo female lead and intially poor green forest male lead

Our Secret Diary

This is a japanese drama based on highschool love story. The plot is popular boy falls for shy girl
